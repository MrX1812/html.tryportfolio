DOCTOR STRANGE IN THE MULTIVERSE OF MADNESS

This is also one of my fev movie of MCU

"Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness" is a 2022 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero of the same name.12 It is a sequel to "Doctor Strange," "Avengers: Endgame," and "WandaVision." The film follows Doctor Strange and a teenage girl from his dreams who can travel across multiverses to battle multiple threats, including other-universe versions of himself, which threaten to wipe out millions across the multiverse.3 The film is directed by Sam Raimi and stars Kevin Feige, Louis D'Esposito, Victoria Alonso, Eric Hauserman, Carroll Scott Derrickson, Jamie Christopher Mitchell Bell, and Richie Palmer.2 Critics have praised the film for its action and visual excitement while taking the franchise in a darker direction

"Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings" is a 2021 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero of the same name.
